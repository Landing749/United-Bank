FAQ

Common
questions.

Everything you need to know. Can't find your answer? Reach out to an admin in-game.

How do I open an account?
Contact an admin in-game or through the admin portal. They'll create your account with your IGN as the username and provide your initial credentials.
How does the credit score work?
Scores range 1–1000. Repaying loans on time adds +60 points per loan. Defaulting removes 120 points. Your balance contributes bonus points at milestones (500, 2000, 5000, 10000 ◆). Higher scores unlock bigger loan limits.
How much can I borrow?
Your limit is determined by your credit score tier AND your balance — whichever is lower applies. For example a Good-tier member (651–750) can borrow up to ◆10,000 OR 3× their balance, whichever is smaller.
Credit vs Debit cards — what's the difference?
Both support loans and have freeze/unfreeze controls. Credit cards charge 10% interest; debit cards charge 15%. Credit cards are better for larger loans. Both are fully secure with fraud monitoring.
What happens if I can't repay?
Contact an admin immediately. Missed payments reduce your score (-120 per default), your card may be frozen, and collateral may be seized. Installment plans are flexible — communicate early for the best outcome.
How do I unfreeze my card?
If you froze your own card, unfreeze directly from your Account Dashboard under the Card tab. If an admin froze it due to suspicious activity or unpaid debt, contact an admin to resolve the issue first.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All account data is stored in Firebase's secure real-time database with enterprise-grade encryption. Your credentials are never shared and every admin action is permanently logged in the audit trail.
Can I have multiple accounts?
No. One account per player (IGN). Multiple accounts will be flagged by our fraud detection system and may result in all accounts being suspended. Your IGN is your identity in the system.

Need admin access?

Admins can access the full command center with account management, loan approvals, fraud detection, and mass operations. All protected with super-admin login.
